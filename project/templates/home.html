PROJECT SUGGESTION
BASED ON CAREER PREDICTION

A Career Prediction and Project Suggestion App is a tool designed to guide individuals,
especially students and professionals, in choosing suitable career paths and projects based on their skills and interests.
This application utilizes a Decision Tree Classifier to analyze various factors, such as academic background, technical expertise, and personal preferences, to predict the most suitable career options.
Additionally, it integrates Google Gemini to suggest relevant projects that align with the predicted career path,
helping users enhance their practical knowledge and improve their employability.

Welcome to our Career Prediction and Project Suggestion App!
Our mission is to empower students and professionals by providing AI-driven career guidance and personalized project recommendations.
Using advanced Decision Tree Classifier algorithms, we analyze your skills, academic background, and interests to predict the best career paths for you.
Additionally, with the help of Google Gemini, we suggest relevant projects that align with your chosen field, helping you gain hands-on experience and enhance your employability.
Our goal is to bridge the gap between learning and career success by offering data-driven insights that make career planning more effective and personalized.
Whether you're a student exploring opportunities or a professional looking to upskill, we are here to guide you every step of the way!
